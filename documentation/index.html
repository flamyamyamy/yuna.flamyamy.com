// Command Directory

⛩️ Anime & Manga Database

Access detailed info from the world of Japanese media.

/anime <title>

Shows a complete overview of an anime, including status, episodes, and a synopsis.

/character <name>

Find information and high-quality images of your favorite anime characters.

/manga <title>

Displays details about manga volumes, chapters, and the publishing story.

👤 User Utilities

Get insights into server members.

/userinfo [user]

View join dates, roles, permissions, and unique Discord IDs.

/avatar [user]

Fetch the full resolution profile picture of a specific user.

/banner [user]

Extract and display a user's profile banner in its original size.

📊 Leveling & Experience

Track your activity and compete with friends.

/rank

Displays your personal rank card with current level and XP progress.

/leaderboard

View the top 10 most active members on the current server.

/level-setup

(Admin Only) Set up XP multipliers and level-up announcement channels.

📜 Inspiration & Quotes

Get inspired by random quotes.

/quote

Brings you a random quote from famous authors or historical figures.

🎮 Social Fun

Interactive features to engage with the community.

/ship <user1> <user2>

Calculates the love compatibility percentage between two users.

/react <user>

Sends a stylish ping with a specific anime reaction image to a friend.

💖 Social Reactions

Express yourself using animated GIF commands.

/react <action> @user

Choose from a wide variety of actions (e.g., hug, pat, slap, kiss) to interact with other users. Just type the command followed by the action and tag a user.
